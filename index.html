💻 How ReadyBoot Works

  1. Select your ISO file
  2. Plug in your USB drive
  3. Click 'Create Bootable USB'

That's it – Bootable USB ready in seconds!

🔧 Development

ReadyBoot is being actively developed by Kuldeep Prajapati to offer a fast, reliable, and user-friendly way to create bootable USB drives from ISO files. Inspired by simplicity and performance, ReadyBoot focuses on:

  • Lightweight design for fast startup and execution
  • Offline functionality with no dependencies on external internet connections
  • Compatibility with a wide range of ISO formats including Windows and Linux distributions
  • Planned integration with license management and user activation via the ReadyBoot Admin App

Development is ongoing, with regular updates aimed at improving performance, stability, and ease-of-use. Suggestions and feedback are always welcome to help shape the future of ReadyBoot.
